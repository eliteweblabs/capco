  • Why diverge from the traditional experience?

  • Traditional vendors subject their customers to three painful problems:

  • 1.

    They offer a patchwork of hardware and software that lacks future-proofing.

    Subsequently, network engineers struggle with interoperability, security, and lifecycle management.

  • 2.

    They focus on selling more hardware, resulting in unpredictable pricing and high software licensing fees.

    Subsequently, network engineers are priced out or must make tough budgetary trade-offs, leading to underprovisioned networks.

    They sell their hardware and leave, requiring highly manual design, installation, management, and support processes.

    Subsequently, network engineers must source multiple vendors or engage in complex deployments and management on their own.

  • How does the switching process work?

  • Kickoff

    The Meter operations team will meet with you to plan the installation. We will set a call to review logistics and your technical requirements.

  • ISP procurement

    Meter can work with your existing ISP or find the best providers to meet your bandwidth needs. We'll negotiate rates on your behalf and handle all ISP customer support for a smooth installation. We are ISP agnostic and partner with every local ISP.

  • Site survey and network design

    Meter will perform a site survey to understand the space and existing infrastructure. Our engineers will create network designs to ensure the best performance and reliability.

  • Legacy hardware buyback

    Meter will buy back legacy hardware to make switching easy and cost-effective. We help you recoup your investments with credits to Meter service and handle all logistics, from pickup to packing.

  • Hardware & cabling installation

    Meter will run low-voltage cabling and install our hardware, which we will ship on-site in advance.

  • Same-day testing & configuration

    Meter’s hardware is pre-configured for your unique network via our digital twin capability.

  • Continuous management & support

    Meter will continue to provide ongoing support and upgrades. We will:

  • Proactively identify and resolve issues on your network.

  • Troubleshoot network issues in real-time.

  • Manage network upgrades and ongoing infrastructure improvements.

  • Network features

  • Network automation

    Meter networks are automated, software-managed, and don't require manual configuration.

  • Multi-site management

    Meter Dashboard enables you to see every single one of your sites, and their health, in one place.

  • Meter Command

    Meter Command allows you to get information about your networks, take action, and generate entire dashboards to fit your workflow, all in natural language.
