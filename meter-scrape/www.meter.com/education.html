Frequently asked questions

View all FAQs
Why do you manufacture your own hardware?

We choose to make our own hardware because we believe a tight integration between hardware and the software that runs on it is required for the best networking experience.

We’re able to improve on our hardware by observing how our customers interact with their networks. We’re also able to improve our customers’ networks more precisely because we know our hardware so well, since we make it ourselves.

How long have you been around?

We started in 2015 to make it really easy for any business to get internet, networking, and Wi-Fi. We're able to do this by vertically integrating the process with software, operations, and hardware.

Meter has over 200 customers across millions of square feet. We are backed by Sam Altman of OpenAI, Sequoia Capital, and Meraki & Samsara founders Sanjit Biswas and John Bicket, amongst others.

What does Meter’s support cover?

Meter handles everything end-to-end, from the ISP to networking devices. If there is an ISP issue, we know immediately through our WAN alerting then contact the ISP on your behalf. If a device sitting on the network has poor connectivity or is down, we’ll root cause the issue to bring it back up and ensure it doesn’t happen again. We work fast and over communicate throughout a support case.

Our goal is to provide you with the outcome of a fast, reliable, and secure network. We do everything to achieve this outcome.

Can I pay for Meter with my E-Rate funds?

If you are a K-12 school in the U.S. eligible for E-Rate, you can pay for Meter under your category 2 budget. We will work with USAC and any E-Rate consultants you employ to get you your refund on the form 471 that you submit.
