Meter named a Visionary in the 2025 Gartner® Magic Quadrant™

Meter has been recognized as a Visionary in the 2025 Gartner® Magic Quadrant™ for Enterprise Wired and Wireless LAN Infrastructure.

This recognition reflects the strength of our new approach. Meter delivers reliable, secure, and scalable full-stack networks. Our solution spans internet, wired, Wi-Fi, and cellular connectivity for the enterprise. We took a different approach from traditional networking by providing an end-to-end solution with our own hardware, software, and operations. It’s subscription-based and delivered for a single fee.
