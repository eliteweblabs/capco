Meter Cellular: reliable cell service—installed in weeks, not months

Published: August 28, 2025
Charlie CobbCellular

Meter Cellular brings the same simplicity and reliability to cell service that we've become known for in networking. We’ve been busy deploying Cellular across diverse environments and refining performance and integration to support enterprises seeking a fast, reliable, and cost-effective cellular solution. Tens of thousands of calls have been made over a Meter Cellular network within the last quarter, and today we’re sharing an early look into our first deployments with MrBeast and Reddit.

Simple integration, comprehensive solution

Cellular is built on neutral host technology, which delivers superior range and performance for in-building cellular coverage. Our technology enables us to broadcast multiple carriers on a single piece of hardware, delivering a solution that’s 5x cheaper to install, manage, and support than traditional distributed antenna system (DAS) alternatives. This means no coaxial cables, no closets of equipment, just fast, scalable coverage.

Full redundancy from client to cloud

E911 reliability is our number one priority. Our infrastructure has no single point of failure.

Meter Cellular vs DAS:

  • 8× Faster day 0 and day 1 than leading DAS solutions
  • 5× Cheaper install, management, and support than leading DAS solutions
  • Minimal coordination and project management required by customers

We've partnered with major US wireless carriers to broadcast their signal through our indoor and outdoor Cellular Access Points, bringing five bars of native coverage to end users’ phones.

Customers seeking an integrated connectivity solution are turning to Meter to provide both Network and Cellular products for seamless coverage throughout their facilities. We’ve completed our first deployments with Reddit, MrBeast, and additional office spaces, warehouses, and schools.

Solving cellular dead zones

Reddit, an existing Meter Network customer, has implemented Cellular at their Santa Monica location.

“Our office in Santa Monica was experiencing connectivity issues with cellular devices. Meter provided the quickest and most convenient solution available while also being cost-effective. I don’t often see that from our partners. It is easy to manage and connects directly to our Meter stack.”
Nick Fohs
Senior Manager of Enterprise Systems and Security

MrBeast is a newly signed Network and Cellular customer—deploying Network across the entire Beast Productions campus, and Cellular in buildings with poor coverage.

“We came to Meter seeking an enterprise-grade, scalable networking solution for our current and expanding campus. Their Cellular solution was a welcome bonus that solved another significant issue of ours: spotty cell coverage.”“Without the added complexity of another cellular solution, Meter established reliable Wi-Fi and Cellular connectivity within weeks, keeping our teams connected and producing engaging content. Our staff members on AT&T and T-Mobile immediately noticed reception bars on their mobile phones.”
Luis Madrigal
CIO

Both deployments reflect what makes Cellular different: fast timelines, seamless integration with our full networking stack, and no added vendor complexity. By tapping into Meter’s experienced Operations Team, we’re able to deploy Cellular in a matter of weeks—an improvement over legacy DAS alternatives that typically take months, or even years, to install.

The future of connectivity

With Cellular, Meter offers a complete connectivity experience for your space. Whether you need ISP management, Wi-Fi, or cell service, we have you covered with one integrated platform, one deployment, and one monthly bill.
