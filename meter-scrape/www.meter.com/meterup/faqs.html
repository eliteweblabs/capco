MeterUp FAQs

General questions

What is MeterUp?

MeterUp is a gathering for business executives, industry analysts, and IT and networking experts to explore and shape the future of networking.

This one-day conference offers a dynamic series of keynotes, fireside chats, and panel discussions, providing a platform for thought-provoking conversations and insightful reflection on the evolving landscape of internet infrastructure. View the agenda at meterup.com/agenda.

Where is MeterUp located?

Pier 27
27 The Embarcadero, San Francisco, CA

When is MeterUp taking place?

MeterUp is taking place on November 18, 2025. It is scheduled to be an all-day event.

Can I attend MeterUp virtually?

The MeterUp keynote will be livestreamed on YouTube.
We will also be recording all speaking sessions and sharing them on-demand, post-event. If you are interested in getting early access to the videos, please email events@meter.com

Registration

What is the cost to attend MeterUp?

The cost to attend MeterUp 2025 is $349 per person.

What payment method do you accept?

Registration must be paid by credit card.

Where can I find my receipt?

A receipt is included with your confirmation email. If you cannot locate your confirmation email, please contact us at events@meter.com for a copy of your receipt.

If I can no longer attend, can I transfer my registration to my colleague?

Yes. Please email events@meter.com and we will be happy to assist you.

What is the cancellation policy?

If you cancel by September 15, we will offer a full refund.

I requested a seat, how do I know I was confirmed?

The Meter team sent a confirmation email to your inbox at the time of confirmation. If you aren't sure, please reach out events@meter.com.

I have food allergies/intolerances. How can I request special food options?

During the registration process, you will be asked for any food allergies/intolerances. If you have any additional specifications or questions, please email events@meter.com

Accessibility

How can I request accessibility accommodations?

Please submit your request to events@meter.com

Will there be gender neutral bathrooms?

Yes, we will have a gender neutral bathroom available.

Will there be a lactation room?

Yes, we will have a lactation room available. Please visit the registration desk and they can direct you to the location.
