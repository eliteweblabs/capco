# Privacy Policy

## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, submit a project, or contact us for support. This may include:

- Name, email address, and contact information
- Company information and project details
- Authentication credentials and preferences
- Communications and support requests

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain our fire protection services
- Process project submissions and manage client accounts
- Communicate with you about your projects and our services
- Improve our website and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

## Cookie Usage

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Our cookie usage includes:

### Essential Cookies
Required for basic website functionality, user authentication, and security.

*These cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function properly.*

### Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

*You can opt out of these cookies through our [cookie preferences](/cookies).*

### Functional Cookies
Enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences.

*These improve your experience but are not essential for basic functionality.*

### Marketing Cookies
Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

*You can opt out of these cookies through our [cookie preferences](/cookies).*

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

- Visiting our [Cookie Preferences](/cookies) page
- Clicking the "Manage Preferences" button in our cookie banner
- Using your browser's cookie settings
- Contacting us directly for assistance

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Access controls and authentication systems
- Secure data storage and backup procedures

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

- The right to access your personal information
- The right to correct inaccurate information
- The right to delete your personal information
- The right to restrict or object to processing
- The right to data portability

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at **privacy@example.com**.
