Scope of Services

The Contractor agrees to provide the following fire protection services:

  • Fire Sprinkler System Design and Installation: Complete design, engineering, and installation of fire sprinkler systems in accordance with NFPA 13 standards.
  • Fire Alarm System Installation: Installation of fire detection and alarm systems meeting NFPA 72 requirements.
  • Fire Extinguisher Placement: Strategic placement and installation of fire extinguishers throughout the facility.
  • Emergency Lighting Systems: Installation of emergency egress lighting systems as required by local codes.
  • Fire Safety Inspections: Annual inspections and maintenance of all installed fire protection equipment.
  • Code Compliance: Ensure all installations meet local, state, and federal fire safety codes and regulations.
Terms and Conditions
1. Payment Terms:

Client agrees to pay 50% of the total contract amount upon signing this agreement. The remaining 50% shall be due upon completion and acceptance of all work. Payment is due within 30 days of invoice date.

2. Timeline:

Work shall commence within 10 business days of contract execution and shall be completed within the agreed timeframe. Any delays caused by factors beyond Contractor's control shall extend the completion date accordingly.

3. Permits and Approvals:

Contractor shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals required for the work. Client shall provide access to the premises and any required documentation.

4. Warranty:

Contractor warrants all work and materials for a period of one (1) year from completion date. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

5. Insurance:

Contractor maintains comprehensive general liability insurance and workers' compensation insurance as required by law.

6. Compliance:

All work shall be performed in accordance with applicable building codes, fire codes, and industry standards including but not limited to NFPA standards.

Liability and Indemnification

Contractor shall not be liable for any damages arising from circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of God, government actions, or Client's failure to provide necessary access or information. Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Contractor from any claims arising from Client's negligence or failure to comply with applicable codes and regulations.

Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. In the event of termination, Client shall pay for all work completed and materials provided up to the date of termination.

Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the state in which the work is performed. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration.
